A Word from Nevmind

Hi, my name is Nevmind (also known as ImNev), and I created this website to support my Discord server and the Civilization events I host. (By the way, I don’t actually know how to code—this was all done through vibe coding.)

What our website offers

Here, you can find forms to sign up for upcoming Civilization events we plan to host, as well as learn about our amazing staff team. You'll also find a few articles and posts about our past events and thoughts on future ones. I'm also planning to develop a bot that will automatically post all my Discord announcements here to keep things connected.

Our approach to civilization events

Unlike many other servers that rely on flashy gimmicks like NPCs and over-the-top mods, we aim to stick to a more standard, vanilla Minecraft experience. We believe the best events aren’t about fighting NPCs or exploring pre-built structures—they’re about creating nations and factions, engaging in diplomacy, political drama, interesting side-stories, and, of course, epic wars.

Help us Continue Hosting Events

Currently, I fund all the server and event costs out of my own pocket. While the expenses aren't huge, there's naturally a limit to what I can cover on my own. That's why I’ve decided to open a Patreon for the server. All money received will go directly toward funding future Civilization events. Patreon supporters will also get some cool perks listed on the page. If you're interested, feel free to check it out through this link: Patreon.
